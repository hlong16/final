Are Covid Restrictions working?

Since the Covid-19 pandemic began, each state in the US has decided what plan they were willing to impliment to combat the pandemic. These plans have varied in there level of seriousness. I set out to find if the states that took more serious steps did a better job at controlling the pandemic than those that did not. To start I found a ranking of the states in order from most restrictive to least restrictive. This ranking took into account 18 different metrics and returned a score from 1 to 100. A higher score indicated less restrictive rules. I then used this ranking along with the covid-19 data from each state to see what results I could infer.
